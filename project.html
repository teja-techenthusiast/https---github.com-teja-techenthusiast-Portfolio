Power Quality Improvement
Led a power quality management project, focusing on harmonics reduction in electric power systems. Implemented a shunt hybrid power filter with advanced d-q and p-q control techniques, integrating an adaptive neuro-fuzzy inference system (ANFIS) for precise real power loss estimation.
